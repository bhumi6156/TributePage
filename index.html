5 APJ Abdul Kalam Quotes That Will Inspire You To Be A Better Person

  • A teacher, scientist and the people's President, APJ Abdul Kalam dedicated his entire life to hard work and public service.
  • The 11th President of India was awarded with the highest civilian honor Bharat Ratna, among many prestigious awards.
  • On his 87th birth anniversary, here are some quotes by the engineer about work, love and life.
  • APJ Abdul Kalam, son of a boat owner, faced many hardships in his childhood. In order to support his family, he started working with his father from an early age. He started selling newspapers to help his family with extra income.
  • Being from a necessitous family, APJ Abdul Kalam understood the value of hard work. During his graduation, his college dean, dissatisfied with his progress on a class project, threatened to revoke his scholarship and set a difficult deadline. Kalam met the deadline and proved hard work can get you anything.
  • APJ Abdul Kalam, a man of simple taste, was keen to develop India. In order to inspire the people of the country, he authored around 25 books. He loved writing poems, one of which he dedicated to his mother. Moreover, he enjoyed spending time at the beach as a child, looking at the waves.
  • APJ Abdul Kalam always believed education enlightens human beings. None of his family members were educated, but he had a strong desire to learn and was persistent in making a difference. During his early years, he was known for being a hardworking and bright student in school. His family used kerosene lamps at night as his small town did not have electricity. He began studying at around 7-8 PM and continued till 11 PM. His mother used to save kerosene to enable him to study at night.
